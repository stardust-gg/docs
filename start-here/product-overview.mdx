### Onboarding Your Players
Onboarding your Players to Web3 begins with **Stardust Profiles**. Each Profile represents a player in your game and comes with a built-in Stardust Wallet to store game inventory on-chain.

Adding **Profile Identifiers** allows you to easily store information on your players, such as social accounts, external wallet addresses, and more. Connect your Web3 gamers and get to know their on-chain identities.

Gain valuable insights into your players' activity and view on-chain stats. Leverage this data to acquire more higher quality players for your game with **Stardust UA**.

<Info>To learn more about growing your game with Stardust UA, please [Contact Us](https://www.stardust.gg/contact).</Info>

### Stardust Custodial Wallets
Store, manage, and intereact with blockchain assets on behalf of your players with **Stardust Wallets**. With simple API calls, developers can sign and send transactions on many different blockchains. Each Stardust Wallet is multi-chain, making it easy to add additional blockchains to your game. 

### Integration

Easily integrate Stardust WaaS using the Stardust Custodial SDK or API. Visit the [Dashboard](https://waas.stardust.gg/login) to generate an API key and get started.
