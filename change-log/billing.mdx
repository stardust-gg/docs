---
title: "Billing FAQ"
---

Stardust has introduced streamlined auto-billing and invoicing functionality setup to make it easy to upgrade the subscription you have with us. 

Here are common questions we receive:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel?">
    Reach out to us directly in order to cancel in line with your signed contract terms.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I have multiple apps created? Will usage across all apps be tracked against my subscription?">
    As long as we have the app IDs, we can associate them all to your billing account. 
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my current contract is based on legacy per-wallet pricing?">
    We will calculate usage-based billing at the end of the month for invoicing. 
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I need multiple email addresses to be sent invoices?">
    Within the Stripe billing dashboard, you will have the option to select what email addresses you would like the invoices sent 
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does auto-billing support ACH?">
    Yes, you can select that as an option when paying in Stripe.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What billing service is Stardust using?">
    Stripe
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
